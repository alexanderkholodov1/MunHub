About MunHub

MunHub

Cosmic Ray Data Platform

v5.0 · Feb 2026

MunHub is a web-based platform for real-time cosmic ray muon detection, data acquisition, storage and visualization. It interfaces with CosmicWatch desktop muon detectors — open-source, affordable particle physics instruments designed for education and research.

The MuNRa Network

MunHub is the software backbone of the MuNRa (Muon Network for Research and Academia) international detector network. The MuNRa network is developed within the EL-BONGO Physics project, funded by the European Union through the Erasmus+ CBHE programme, connecting universities across Latin America and Europe for collaborative cosmic ray research.
